TÄNAK WINS WRC TOP DRIVER AWARD FOR SECOND SEASON.

The Estonian was voted WRC Driver of the Year for a second consecutive season by fans on the FIA World Rally Championship’s official wrc.com website. Tänak claimed a popular maiden victory in Italy and landed a second success in Germany en route to a third place championship finish in M-Sport’s Ford Fiesta World Rally Car. His co-driver and fellow countryman, Martin Järveoja, was named WRC Co-driver of the Year following an impressive debut WRC season with Tänak.
