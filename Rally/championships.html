Monte-Carlo

Since its inception in 1911, this race that competes on asphalt and snow and under difficult conditions, has an important role in testing the latest improvements and innovations for cars. The one who wins the race acquires great reputation and publicity. Since 1973, the race has been held in January, and around 1,461 kilometers are covered in 15 stages.

Sweden

The Swedish Rally, officially Swedish Rally, is a rally competition that takes place annually in Värmland, Sweden at the beginning of February since 1950. It is rated since 1973 for the World Rally Championship, being absent in 1994 when it was scored for the championship of 2 liters and in 2009 having been replaced by the Rally of Norway. This year has 18 stages.

Mexico

The Rally Mexico, also known as the Rally of Mexico and officially the Rally Guanajuato Mexico, is a rally event held annually in Mexico, date of the FIA World Rally Championship that entered the championship calendar in Mexico. 2004 and is currently the only event in North America in the championship. The itinerary of the event is based in the state of Guanajuato. The stages take place around the cities of León, Silao, Irapuato and Guanajuato. The main offices of the event are in León.
