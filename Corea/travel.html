How to get to South Korean?

Currently, due to international relations with North Korea, you can only access South Korea by PLANE or SHIP. It is necessary to present the passport at the border to access the country.



We recommend landing at the following airports:

City of Seoul

  • - International Airport of Incheon
  • - International Airport of Gimpo

City of Daegu

  • - International Airport of Daegu

City of Yangyang

  • - International Airport of Yangyang

If you decide to come by sea, we can recommend the following ports:

Gangneung

  • - Gangneung Marina

Province of Gyeonggi

  • - Seoul Marina, Seoul
  • - Jeongok Marina, Ansan

City of Yangyang

  • - Susan Marina
